Welcome to Munki!

The Munki Project is home to several projects that are focused on improving the experience of managing OS X for both the admin and the end user. Munki is surrounded by a vibrant community that is passionate about the craft of managing OS X and the services that surround it.

Community

Part of what makes Munki so popular is the fantastic community that surrounds it. If you want to get involved in the community, you can join the mailing list, pop into the #munki channel on the MacAdmins Slack, or come to one of many Mac Admin conferences around the world.
